ACHIEVMENTS

ACCS-ARM Design Challenge

Team comprising of Eldhose Joy, Varghese Mathai and Arun Baby, under the guidance of Asst. Prof Anas M.M, bagged the third prize of Rs 40,000, for their work on “Zone Adaptive Warning and Speed Control System”in the finals of 3rd National Level ACCS-ARM Design challenge

SYM STARTUP GOT AQUIHIRED

Save Your Money(SYM), a fintech startup has been acquired by Accel Partners backed Good Methods Global(GMG). SYM was founded by Aravind Muraleedharan,Vivek S,Abin Alias,Aswin Dev and Sarah Abraham while in their third year of engineering at MITS. Hearty congratulations to team SYM.

ACCS-ARM Design Challenge

The team led by KPS Sachin, Athul Stanley and Abhijith A Sabu and mentored by Prof. Anas M.M received the honour of ‘Most Promising Project’ in the 3rd National Level ACCS-ARM Design for their effort On “Driving Control System Based on Heart Beat” and won Rs. 25,000/- for their project.

ACCS-ARM Design Challenge

ACTIVITIES

SOCIAL

Induction Training
June 2018

Induction Training for juniors is to welcome them and organize an interactive session on App-inventor for them.

Hour Of Code
Jume & July 2018

Hour of code is a program conducted at schools.This is teaching students a new technology by our college students of computer scence department to promote school students to have an idea and know the basics of the technology such as App-Inventor, Android,etc.We are doing it for free of cost.

Empowering housewives
August 2018

Empowerment of women is a necessity for the very development of a society,since it enhances both the quality and quantity of human resources available for development.The idea of empowering house wives is actually teach the house wives nearby our college,the basics,on and how to use a smartphone and a computer. This idea will be executed by girls.

Workshop
August 2018

For the student a workshop is planned based on current trend. It is conducted as a intra-college event so, students from other department can also participate in this workshop.

TECHNICAL

Quiz
September 2018

A technical quiz competition is planned for encouraging and promoting students to show their technical knowledge on a special course.It helps them to understand their level on technical fields and could help them to focus more on that.Our faculties will judge the program.

Ideathon
September 2018

An ideathon for girls is basically promoting girls of our college to actively participate in technical events and encourage them to develop new ideas.These event consist of a 9Hr process.The first 6Hr is for preparing anew solution for the problem statement given by the event committee,with a small prototype(if required),implementation details (not necessary to implement),business viability,social benefits,etc.Then after these preparation time,each team has to present their ideas in front of a qualified panel of judges,who will be mainly our faculties.This event is open to whole branches.

Blind Coding
October 2018

Blind coding is for students with basic knowledge on computer language .Students can participate in a team of two.They will be give a simple basic C program to code with the computer monitor off.This event is a fun plus evaluating the coding skill ofstudents.

Technical Talk
October-November 2018

For students of computer science a technical talk is planned by some eminent person from industry and an Interactive section by Pass out students.It is an interactive section where students can interact with the person about the topic he speaks.
